## What is Connections?

Connections is this daily brain teaser from The New York Times that I’m absolutely hooked on! 😍 Every day, you’re handed 16 words, and your mission—should you choose to accept it—is to group them into four categories of four. Sounds easy, right? Trust me, it’s a wild ride! 🚀 Just when you think you’ve got it all figured out, there’s always that one word that throws you off. The puzzle starts simple but cranks up the difficulty with each level, so you better bring your A-game! 🧠💪

And guess what? You only get four chances to mess up, so be smart with your picks! Once you solve it, don’t forget to share your results and brag to your friends. It’s the best kind of addiction, promise! 🎮✨
