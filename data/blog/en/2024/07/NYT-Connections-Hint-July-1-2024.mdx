## Word Explanations

- **DIRECT**: To manage or guide by advice, helpful information, instruction, etc.
- **GUIDE**: To assist someone to travel through or reach a destination in an unfamiliar area.
- **LEAD**: To show the way to a destination by going in front or beside.
- **USHER**: To escort or guide someone to a seat or place.
- **CON**: A scheme or trick designed to deceive or defraud someone.
- **HUSTLE**: A fraudulent scheme or swindle.
- **RACKET**: An illegal or dishonest scheme for obtaining money.
- **STING**: A complicated confidence game planned and executed with great care.
- **EAGLE**: A symbol featured on the Great Seal of the United States, seen on the dollar bill.
- **PYRAMID**: A geometric figure featured on the back of the U.S. dollar bill.
- **SEAL**: Refers to the Great Seal of the United States, depicted on the dollar bill.
- **WASHINGTON**: The first U.S. President, whose portrait appears on the one-dollar bill.

- **NORTHWEST**: The first word in the name of the Canadian territory Northwest Territories.

- **PRINCE**: The first word in the name of the Canadian province Prince Edward Island.
